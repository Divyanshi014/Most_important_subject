How To Mould Your Children In DCIM

Teach them about online safety

It is important to educate children about the risks and dangers of the internet, such as cyberbullying, identity theft, and online predators. Teach them to keep their personal information private and to only communicate with trusted individuals online.

Encourage responsible online behavior

It is important to teach children to be respectful and responsible when using the internet. This includes not sharing inappropriate content, not engaging in cyberbullying or online harassment, and being mindful of the impact their online actions can have on others.

Offer DCIM education

Incorporate DCIM education into the curriculum and provide resources and support to parents to help them educate their children about responsible internet use.
